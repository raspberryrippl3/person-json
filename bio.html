Business Description

Oliver Partridge

Hi! I'm Oliver Partridge, a web developer at OI Digital. My journey in developing websites began during the height of the dot-com bubble circa 2000, working for a magazine publisher building websites to drive online subscriptions. That's 24 years in the business and I truly have seen it all - the good, the bad, and the ugly. A lot has changed in the way websites get built and developed in those 24 years and I take the time to make sure I am up-to-speed with current website build methods, web technologies and web design trends.

I love my job. I have worked for a diverse range of clients, from the NHS, the RSPCA, to your local builder or plumber. But it's not only that, I love my clients and have built relationships out of them. Quite often I can be found cycling through the Chilterns with a Product Strategist!
